Recent Disaster Strategies in India

Efforts and measures taken to manage and mitigate recent disasters.

Flood Management in Bihar (2024)

In response to severe flooding in Bihar, the state government has implemented advanced flood forecasting systems, improved embankments, and organized community awareness programs to enhance preparedness and response.

Earthquake Preparedness in Himachal Pradesh (2024)

Following recent seismic activities, Himachal Pradesh has strengthened its earthquake-resistant infrastructure, conducted regular drills, and provided training for local emergency responders to ensure effective management of earthquake impacts.

COVID-19 Pandemic Response (2024)

The Indian government has continued its efforts in combating COVID-19 by expanding vaccination drives, enhancing healthcare infrastructure, and implementing stricter health protocols to prevent the spread of the virus.
